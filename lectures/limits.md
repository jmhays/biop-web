Consider a function $$\frac{\cos x - x}{3x^2}$$. What is the limit as $$x\rightarrow 0$$?

L'Hopital:

$$\lim_{x\rightarrow 0}\frac{-\sin x}{6x} = \lim_{x\rightarrow 0}\frac{-\cos x}{6} = -\frac{1}{6}$$

Not zero!
